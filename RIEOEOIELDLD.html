Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. OSINTDOG.COM is committed to protecting your data and maintaining complete transparency about our practices.

Data We DON'T Store

We do NOT store, log, or retain:

  • Your search queries or any content you search for
  • Your IP addresses or location data
  • Browsing history or session details
  • Personal identifiers beyond what's necessary for operation
  • Search results or any data retrieved through our APIs

Data We Store (Minimal & Necessary)

We only store essential information required for basic service operation:

  • API Keys: Securely encrypted and stored only for authentication
  • Payment Information: Processed through secure third-party providers (we don't store credit card details)
  • Basic Usage Metrics: Anonymous request counts for service optimization and billing
  • Account Email: For account management and support communication only

Zero Data Retention Policy

Our platform operates on a zero data retention principle. All search queries and results are processed in real-time and immediately discarded. We never build profiles, track usage patterns, or store any intelligence data that passes through our system.

Third-Party Services

We integrate with various OSINT APIs and data sources. These third-party services may have their own privacy policies. We ensure that no personal data is shared with these services beyond what's necessary for query processing.

Data Security

All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard TLS protocols. API keys are stored using advanced encryption methods, and our infrastructure follows security best practices to protect the minimal data we do store.

Contact Us: If you have any privacy concerns or questions about our data practices, please reach out through our contact form. We're committed to transparency and will address any privacy-related inquiries promptly.
